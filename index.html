Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is XPanova??

    Xpanova is a leading e-commerce company that offers a wide range of services, including online shopping, website designing, website development, blogger custom services, reseller customer care, e-commerce brand consultation, basic visual programming, and reselling. We are committed to helping businesses of all sizes succeed online.

  • What kind of products do you sell?

    We sell a wide range of products, including electronics, clothing, home goods, and more. We source our products from trusted suppliers and offer competitive prices. We also offer a variety of shipping options to meet your needs.

  • How do I place an order?

    To place an order, simply click'Check Price' button on product page and it will redirect you to our whatsapp number. There you ask availablity and place your order.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept UPI, all major debit and credit cards. We also accept Paypal for international customers.

  • How much is shipping?

    Currently, we are offering free shipping on all over india. For international shipping, shipping fees will be applicable according to the courier partner.

  • When will I receive my order?

    Most orders are shipped within 24-48 hours and arrive within 5-10 business days. In case of any technical issues, order may delay. In such scenarios, you can contact our customer care service

  • What is your return policy?

    Please note that all claims for damage, defects, or incorrect items must be made on the same day of delivery. You must also submit an unboxing video as required.

    Unboxing video rules:

    • Record a 360-degree unboxing video of the product, showing the defect, damage, or issue in the same video without pausing.

      We will not be able to issue a refund or replacement without an unboxing video.

    Electronic products, such as smartwatches:

    • For electronic products, turn on the device and demonstrate the issue. If the device is out of battery, plug it into the charger without pausing the video and wait two minutes for it to charge.

      Please try to record the unboxing video with the assistance of another person.

    Replacement:

    • If you receive a defective product or incorrect item and submit the required unboxing video, you will be eligible for a replacement.

      If available, we will arrange a return pickup. If return pickup is not available, we will provide you with a return address to our warehouse. You must then send the product to us via courier.

      We will refund your courier charges if the error is ours. If the error is not ours, you must pay the courier charges yourself.

    Refund:

    • Refunds are only available in specific circumstances. For example, if the product is out of stock for replacement, you may request a refund.

      If the error is ours, we will refund you 100% of the amount and cover your return courier charges.

      If the error is not ours, you must return the product at your own expense.

    Please do not hesitate to contact us if you have any further questions.
